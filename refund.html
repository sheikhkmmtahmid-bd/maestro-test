1. Eligibility for Refund

After receiving and confirming a Purchase Order (PO), the client will be eligible for a refund under the conditions set out in this policy.

2. Refund Process

If the client wishes to terminate the Contract prior to the end of the Primary Tenure without any non-compliance of any part of the Contract, the Client shall initiate the same by sending a 180 (one hundred and eighty) days' written notice, along with a deposit of 50% of the total remaining Monthly Recurring Charges (MRCs) until the end of the Primary Tenure, in favour of the service provider.

Please contact our customer service team at info@maestro.com.bd or +880 1713 336 401 with your tracking number, invoice number, or PO number and details of the issue.

Our team will review your request and notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund. If approved, the refund will be processed and credit will automatically be applied to your original method of payment within 7 to 10 business days.

3. Late or Missing Refunds

If you have not received your approved refund within 10 business days of initiation, please first check your bank account and contact your card issuer or mobile financial service (MFS) provider, as processing times may vary. If you have done all of this and still have not received your refund, please contact us at info@maestro.com.bd.
