Why We Certify

Three Pillars of Our Compliance

Our certifications span three critical dimensions that define responsible and sustainable garment manufacturing.

Production Excellence

ISO 14001, Higg Index, GOTS, OCS, and RCS certifications verify that our production processes meet stringent environmental and quality management standards throughout the entire supply chain.

Ethical Compliance

Certifications from RSC, Amfori/BSCI, SEDEX, WRAP, and EcoVadis confirm that our factory meets international standards for labour rights, health and safety, and responsible business conduct.

Worker Welfare

OEKO-TEX Standard 100, Cotton Made in Africa, and our social compliance audits demonstrate our unwavering commitment to the health, safety, and dignity of every worker in our facility.

Social Responsibility

Compliance Goes Beyond Paper

Our certifications are backed by genuine on-the-ground action. From healthcare programmes to environmental initiatives, we embed responsibility into every aspect of our operations. Explore our sustainability page to learn more.

Environmental Management

Tree plantation drives, rainwater harvesting, and a 300 KW solar energy target demonstrate our proactive approach to environmental stewardship.

Employee Welfare

Free healthcare services, fire safety training, anti-harassment policies, and skills development programmes are integral to our workforce strategy.

Community Engagement

We actively participate in community development programmes, supporting education, health awareness, and emergency relief in and around Gazipur.

Compliance You Can Count On

Source with confidence from a manufacturer that holds 12 international certifications and audits its practices continuously.
