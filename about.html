Who We Are

Maestro Solutions Ltd.

Maestro Solutions Ltd. is a well-established software company founded in the year 2000 by a group of passionate engineers driven by a single vision: to bring world-class, purpose-built technology solutions to Bangladesh's emerging internet industry.

What has always distinguished Maestro is our deliberate choice to pursue the path less travelled — the areas of genuine challenge, deep research, and meaningful innovation. This philosophy is why our solutions stand apart: they are not off-the-shelf adaptations, but original, ground-up creations designed specifically for the needs of the Bangladeshi ISP market.

Maestro has been the pioneer in Radius-based ISP Billing since 2001. Between 2001 and 2007, twenty-four ISPs across Bangladesh relied on our MaXim ISP Billing System for their prepaid and postpaid dial-up customers. In 2007, we achieved a landmark milestone by developing Bangladesh's first-ever broadband billing system, deployed for Smile (BDCOM). In 2016, we launched a completely redesigned MaXim ISP Billing platform — purpose-engineered for the demands of today's ISP business landscape.

We were also the first to deliver a BTRC-compliant IP Log Server when ISPs across the country faced a critical compliance deadline with no viable solution available. Working in close collaboration with law enforcement authorities, Maestro developed a cost-effective, fully approved system that continues to serve hundreds of ISPs today. Over more than two decades, we have served major telecommunications companies including Teletalk and BTCL, manufacturing conglomerates, group companies, and international clients — earning our position as Bangladesh's most trusted ISP software partner.
