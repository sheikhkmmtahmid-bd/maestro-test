Who We Are

Maestro Solutions Ltd.

Maestro Solutions Ltd. is a well-established software company founded in the year 2000 by a group of enthusiastic engineers who shared a vision: to bring world-class technology solutions to Bangladesh's emerging internet industry.

Over more than two decades, we grew into the number-one provider of IP Log Server and ISP Billing solutions in Bangladesh. Our products have been deployed at major telecommunications companies including Teletalk and BTCL, manufacturing groups, and international clients across Asia.

Today, we continue to innovate — delivering four core product lines that address every major operational challenge faced by ISPs and internet enterprises in Bangladesh and beyond.
